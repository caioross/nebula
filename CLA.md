## Why a CLA

The AGPL keeps Nebula free for everyone. The CLA exists for one reason: it lets the project act as a single copyright holder — to enforce the license against violators, to fix licensing mistakes, and to keep long-term options open without hunting down every past contributor for permission. Your work stays available under the AGPL permanently; clause 2 guarantees it.

## How to sign

Signing is handled per pull request. Add the following line to the description of your first pull request:

```
I have read the CLA at CLA.md and I agree to its terms.
Signed: <your name> <your email>
```

The agreement covers that contribution and all future ones under the same identity.
